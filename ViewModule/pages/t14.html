Learning Outcome(s):

The two previous sections demonstrated how to achieve relevancy, adequate focus, and proper exposure in forensic photography. In this section you will practice putting images in the proper order to ensure relevancy.

Determine where each image would be in order from 1 to 16. Click the drop-down to make a selection and then check to confirm the choice. Click on each image to see it in greater detail.

Remember to consider linkage between photographs, and think about how you might plan out the order before you begin taking photographs. Before hitting submit, look through the order you have chosen and see if the photographs ‘tell the story’ of the scene, and fall in the correct sequence.
