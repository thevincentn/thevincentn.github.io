Introduction

Learning Outcome(s):

  1. Explain the purpose of forensic photography.
  2. Explain the image requirements for effective forensic photography.

Forensic photography refers to documenting scenes, people, and items by means of photography for the purpose of using the images in the courts. What sets forensic photography apart from regular photography is the legal requirement for an accurate and unbiased visual record of a crime scene and/or evidence.
