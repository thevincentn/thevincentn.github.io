Digital Evidence Management System (DEMS)

The Digital Evidence Management System (DEMS) is a joint project between the BC Police Agencies, BC Prosecution Service and PRIME Corp to identify a system where police can collect and store evidence and transmit it to Crown Counsel. DEMS provides a central and secure storage cloud. It replaces your network folders as a place to store digital evidence like photographs, audio recordings and scanned documents. Evidence.com

In 2021, Vancouver Police Department's (VPD) Beat Enforcement Team (BET) piloted the Axon system for four months along with partner agencies like the RCMP. After a successful pilot, the VPD proceeded with rolling out mobile devices and Axon products for cloud based electronic disclosure department wide.

The Axon Capture mobile app and Evidence.com desktop dashboard was used by patrol to collect, store, and transmit digital evidence to Crown. The pilot of this system was supported by forensic photography training provided by the Forensic Identification Unit. This eLearning module has been built based on that training.

Axon Capture

Axon Capture is a mobile app that allows you to perform the following actions:

  • Capture audio and photos.
  • Categorize files with case ID, title and a retention category.
  • Upload audio and photo evidence captured with the app or saved on the mobile device to Evidence.com, using either a cellular data connection or Wi-Fi Internet connection.
  • Enable public submission of evidence.
Evidence.com

Evidence.com is a website that provides a secure and easily accessed interface for management, sharing and viewing of mission critical data. Microsoft Edge is the preferred browser for Evidence.com.
