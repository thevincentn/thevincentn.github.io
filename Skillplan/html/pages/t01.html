Introduction

This eLearning course provides late term apprentices, journeyworkers, mid-level administration and owners with leadership skills and strategies for mentoring workers on the jobsite. By the end of this module, you will:

Click on each to learn more.

Recognize the responsibilities as a mentor and leader on-the-job.
Improve improve skills transference and productivity of apprentices and new workers while maintaining a safe working environment.
Adopt six-step approach for teaching skills that includes techniques for demonstrating, evaluating and providing feedback for the worker learning new skills.
