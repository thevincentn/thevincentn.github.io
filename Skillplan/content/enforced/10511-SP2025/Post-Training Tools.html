About this Resource

Now that you've completed the Indigenous Awareness Training, you’re ready to put your learning into practice. To support this next step, we’ve developed a series of post-training tools that reinforce the course content and help you apply key concepts on the job.

These tools are part of the federally funded Industry Training Champions (ITC) Project, which supports both workers and leaders in strengthening skills and building more inclusive workplaces in the construction industry.

About the Industry Training Champions (ITC) Project

The ITC Project is designed to maximize learning transfer and skill application in the workplace. It provides dual-focused training for both workers and leaders, integrating Skills for Success with the history of Indigenous Peoples and situations happening in the construction industry today.

What are Skills for Success?

The Government of Canada’s Skills for Success framework identifies nine skills that are critical to success in a rapidly changing world.

Indigenous Awareness Training targets four skills:

  • Communication
  • Collaboration
  • Adaptability
  • Problem Solving

These skills are developed and applied through practical workplace scenarios, equipping participants with career development tools to strengthen relationships with Indigenous coworkers, employees and communities.

Post-Training Reinforcement Tools

To reinforce the online course learning, there are three post-training tools:

 Follow-up Email

A reminder of key takeaways and additional resources to support continued learning, sent automatically one to two weeks after course completion.

Access for Free

 Toolbox Talks

Brief, on-the-job meetings to reinforce learning in a practical setting.

Access for Free

 Debrief Session

A structured group discussion to reflect on key learnings from the online course and explore real-world applications, adaptable in length and content for organization-specific needs.

Access for Free

Why Reinforcement Matters

Learning doesn't stop when the course ends. These tools are designed to support the transfer of learning—the process of turning knowledge into action. Without reinforcement, it’s easy for new ideas to fade. With these tools, you’ll be better equipped to:

  • Reflect on and internalize key concepts
  • Share insights with your team
  • Translate learning into meaningful workplace practices
  • Stay engaged in reconciliation and cultural inclusion efforts

Benefits for Your Organization

By taking part in the ITC Project, your organization gains access to practical, cost-free resources that promote lasting change, including:

  • High-quality online training for both workers and leaders
  • Post-training tools to reinforce and apply learning
  • Enhanced interpersonal and intercultural skills across your team
  • Impact measurement tools (pre- and post-training surveys)
  • Custom reports for organizations with 30+ participants
  • Progress toward equity, inclusion, and reconciliation goals
