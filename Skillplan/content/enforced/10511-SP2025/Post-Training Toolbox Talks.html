About this Resource

These short toolbox talks are designed to reinforce key messages from the Indigenous Awareness course your crew has already completed. They're designed for timely relevance and bring course concepts into real jobsite conversations.

Overview

Meeting Details:

  • All participants should have completed the online Indigenous Awareness course.
  • Each talk takes about 5 minutes.
  • Designed to be delivered by a jobsite leader as part of regular safety meetings.

Each talk includes a few discussion questions and key points to guide conversation. You don’t need to be an expert — just ask the questions, give people a chance to respond and use the talking points provided. These sessions are meant to be short, interactive and respectful.

By keeping these conversations going on-site, you’re helping to build a more informed, respectful and inclusive workplace.
