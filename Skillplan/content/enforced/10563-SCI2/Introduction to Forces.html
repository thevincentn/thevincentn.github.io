Introduction to Forces

Understanding how force is essential knowledge for anyone in the trades. Whether you're lifting materials, operating tools or installing systems, you're constantly working with forces. Knowing how they behave can help you make safer choices, troubleshoot problems and avoid costly (or dangerous) mistakes.

In this module, you will learn about force and how it influences other physical phenomenon. By the end, you’ll have a solid understanding of how force shows up on the job.
