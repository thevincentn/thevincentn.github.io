Course Rationale

Sections 489.1 to 490.1 of the Criminal Code (1985) create a statutory framework for the detention, disposal, forfeiture and return of seized property. In accordance with these sections, a Form 5.2 - Report to Justice shall be completed any time property is seized by an officer for an investigation or legal process. To further the detention of seized property, an officer will also be required to fill section 490 forms.

The Report to a Justice Forms eLearning Course presents an overview of these forms. The course provides information, instructions, and all forms necessary to comply with the Criminal Code (1985) with respect to property. The course will look at their implications within a legal context along with various Vancouver Police Department (VPD) policies. The course will also provide an overview of how they are to be filled, how they can be accessed, and common problems which can arise.
